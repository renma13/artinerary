About the Journal!

The Artinerary is a downloadable (and printable!) journal for artists to use and help them be more organized with their personal projects and commissions! Its contents have been consulted and reviewed by professional artists for accuracy and validity.

How to Use

After downloading the journal, you can choose to print it as a hard copy or keep it digital. You're free to personalize the journal however you'd like by using stickers and doodling on pages! Once you're satisfied with how it looks, you can start your journal journey! Enjoy!
