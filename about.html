About the Creators!

The Artinerary journal is a project created by a group MMA Students from De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde as their Thesis project.

Based on the results of their research “The Common Misconceptions of Digital Illustration”, they found out there was a disconnection between the artist and the commissioners. In order to address this, the creators made the Artinenary Journal to provide artists with a medium to track the progress of their projects and commissions.
