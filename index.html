What's the Artinerary?

Based on the results of a research conducted by us, we made the Artinerary journal to provide artists with a medium to track the progress of their projects and commissions, as well as help them price their work in a much more intricate manner.

Made for Artists by Artists

The Artinerary was made specifically for Digital Artists, both beginner and veterans alike! The contents were based on research and consulted with professional artists who do commissions themselves for the most accurate and helpful information.

Why download it!

The Artinerary is a downloadable (and printable!) journal for artists to use and help them be more organized with their personal projects and commissions! Its contents have been consulted and reviewed by professional artists for accuracy and validity.

Start your art journey!

Aim higher as an artist with our journal! We hope that this project can encourage and guide you whether you are beginning or continuing your journey as an artist :)
